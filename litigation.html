Litigation Services

Our Law Firm has a vast experience in litigation, law suits and settlement and provides reliable, sufficient and professional services to a wide range of clients individuals and legal entities ranging from small enterprises to big companies and corporations.

Our litigation team handles cases in all areas of law but the majority of our work is in the areas of Commercial and Personal Injury Disputes.

Our Team will deal with your dispute professionally, efficiently and dynamically and is able to undertake any possible way of resolving of any legal matter, including different forms of alternative dispute resolution.

Before we give any advice regarding any legal action we consider very carefully all the aspects and we analyse any risk involved in connection with all the necessary expenses.

We are pursuing and defending our client’s rights and needs and we offer practical and acceptable solutions.

Our Law Firm has extensive experience in Commercial Litigation, Personal Injury Disputes, Employment Disputes, Medical Negligence Disputes and Property Disputes and we are always adopting flexible, creative and constructive approach, tailored to the needs of the client and the complexities of the dispute in question.

We assist our clients with:

  • Risk Analysis;
  • Obtaining interim orders;
  • Enforcement;
  • Recognition and Enforcement of Foreign Judgements;
  • Debt Collection;
