Our Offices

Our Law Firm is located in Paphos, a coastal city in the southwest of Cyprus, an important business destination and the second largest Southern resort of the island.

The law firm P. Kourides & Co LLC is located in the business and financial centre of Paphos, our offices are conveniently located near to the District Court of Paphos, Tax Office, Land Registry Office and other Government Offices and important Landmarks.

Please find the below tips on how to easily find us:

Across the road from the District Court of Paphos there is a coffee shop “Arabica” our office is situated down on the same road on the opposite side from the Arabica coffee shop in the Aristo Center building. Our offices are on the second floor No. 203-204. Kindly note if you are travelling by car, that the road on which the coffee shop is located is a one-way road and you cannot take the turn from the main road. If the court building is on your left side, the Arabica coffee shop is on your right side, please take the next turn right, go straight on, you will see the Aristo Center on your right.

Please do not hesitate to contact us if you require further information!
