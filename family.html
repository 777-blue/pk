Family Law

Family law commonly raises highly emotional issues which can bring one of the most distressing moments in the lives of any client, thus we are committed to handling all matters tactically and with the utmost sensitivity in a non-confrontational and constructive way.

We have helped countless clients through Family Law matters, providing compassionate advice and passionate advocacy.

We take the time to carefully listen to our clients, in order to understand their individual circumstances.

Our goal is to provide our clients with the legal information and advice necessary to make the best decisions and to reduce the strain on the clients by providing a reliable service and advice in which our clients have absolute confidence.

We understand that family law matters are highly sensitive and often emotional in nature and we handle all such matters with discretion and care offering first class legal advice which is considerate and effective.

Our approach to Family Law is to strive for the best possible outcome while minimizing disruption in the lives of our clients.

We have earned a reputation for delivering excellent legal advice to clients in difficult situations, and we pride ourselves on tailoring our representation to their specific needs.

We apply an approach of creative problem solving to our client’s family law issues, striving to seek solutions which are successful and represent value for money for our clients.

We maintain absolute confidentiality over any matter, or potential matter, our clients can have absolute confidence in our discretion.

We are experienced and able to advise any clients on any family law issues:

  • Marriage
  • Divorce proceedings
  • Custody issues
  • Division of assets
  • Adoption
