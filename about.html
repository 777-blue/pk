ABOUT US

Dr. Panikos Kourides, the founder and Principal Advocate of P. Kourides & Co LLC, is a graduate of the Law Faculty of the Leipzig University in Germany and has been a member of the Cyprus Bar Association since 1991 and a member of the Cypriot Society of Sciences since April 1990.

With over 27 years of experience in various areas of the Law, Dr. Panikos Kourides established his own Law Firm P. Kourides & Co LLC in 2008 in Paphos.

Dr. Panikos Kourides is the leader of a team of qualified Lawyers and administration staff and together with International Associates, and leading European Law Firms and Legal Networks across Europe and the world provides with high professional standards a full range of professional legal services and communication skills required to perform in the local as well as international business environment.

Our Vision and Philosophy:

  1. Each case of any of our clients is important to us.
  2. We are dedicated to the needs of our clients and we offer close personal contact that is specific to their individual needs and circumstances.
  3. We provide timely and rational solutions with delivery of service of the highest quality based on our long experience and deep knowledge of the legal and business environments.
  4. We protect your interests and needs with independence, integrity and confidentiality which is based on confidence and trust.
