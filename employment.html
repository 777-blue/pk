Employment Law

With our commercial and litigious experience, we are able to offer a full-service and comprehensive range of innovative tools to help individuals as well as corporate clients on all Employment Law matters.

Our specialized team advices and represents individuals as well as corporations on the full spectrum of Employment Laws and regulations applicable in Cyprus.

We provide advisory services and litigation representation, we draft relevant documentation and advice on Employment Law matters.

We maintain vast expertise in general employment matters and a specific expertise in employment law fields.

Our core advisory work covers employment agreements, employee governance advice, human resources advisory matters, redundancies, the acquisition of employment permits or reunification permits for companies and ancillary maters in relation to employment laws and regulations.

Our core litigation representation covers dismissal cases, cases involving the enforcement of employment undertakings, redundancies and other ancillary litigation.

We inform our clients as employers on their obligations to their employees, on matters such as unfair dismissal and discrimination, we draft employment agreements and handbooks, provide guidance and assist in the events of Redundancy.

Our Team offers professional, cost-effective guidance and defence in the areas of:

  • Employment issues
  • Drafting of Agreements / Documents
  • Employment handbooks, policies and practices
  • Employment Representation
  • Self-Employment and Consultancy
  • Employment Dispute Resolution
  • Discrimination
  • Dismissal
  • Harassment

We provide our employer and employee clients with expert legal advice on different issues relating to Employment Law.

Employment raises a number of unique and complex matters and when problems do occur they can be disruptive and also expensive.

We provide efficient and cost-effective services with a pragmatic approach, helping our clients to balance employment with the wider commercial aspects which affect the business decisions they make.

Our goal is to provide speedy, exact advice which is accurate, important and as cost effective as possible.
