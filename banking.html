Banking and Finance

As requirements in the financial industry are getting more and more demanding and complex, we are here to provide our legal advice and expertise in banking and finance matters. We have experience in helping clients to navigate the Cyprus legal system across the whole spectrum of banking and finance transactions, such as:

  • Commercial / Private bank account opening
  • Loan and security transactions
  • Restructuring of existing loans and collateral
  • Refinancing of existing debts
  • Legal opinions and legal due diligence

Besides the above our law firm provides its clients with legal advice on all matters of Banking Law including matters of insolvency and bankruptcy.

Our goal is to provide the most beneficial solutions to our clients in order to achieve their own goals.
